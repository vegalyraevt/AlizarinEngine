3.  **Standard Audio Post-Processing:** You are permitted to use standard DAW effects (e.g., reverb, EQ, compression, distortion, auto-tune) to process and alter the voice output for your projects.

* **Q: What's the difference between "Commercial Music" (Allowed) and "Commercial IP Use" (Prohibited)?**
